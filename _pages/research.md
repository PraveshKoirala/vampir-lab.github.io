# Research

Ongoing research includes:
- Solving Equilibrium Problems with Equilibrium Constraints
- Solving for Equilibrium points of Quadratic Program Networks
- Handling constraints in mixed-strategy games
- Investigating the theoretical and practical function approximation power of ReLU neural networks.
- Developing software for trajectory optimization of legged robots
- Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning for Cyber Security
